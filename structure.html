Low-carb Flammkuchen

Description

This dish is a low-carb / keto friendly interpretation of Flammkuchen, a dish native to the Alsace region of France.

Total Ingredients

  • Preheat Oven to 425 degrees Farenheit
  • Parchment Paper
  • Cooking Spray or Butter
  • 3 cups of Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 cup of Almond Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tbsp of Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 lb. of Bacon
  • 2 Small Yellow/White Onions
  • 1/4 cup of Sour Cream

Bacon and Onions
  • 1/2 lb. of Bacon
  • 2 Small Yellow/White Onions
  • 1/4 cup of Sour Cream
  1. Cut bacon into thin 1/4"-1/2" x 1" strips
  2. Slice onions
  3. Cook bacon until it begins to crisp and brown
  4. Set aside cooked bacon and drain most of the fat
  5. Brown onions in the remaining bacon grease
  6. Turn off heat and mix bacon, onions, and sour cream

Dough
  • 2 cups of Mozzarella
  • 1 cup of Almond Flour
  • 3 Tbsp. of Cream Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  1. Add mozzarella, almond flour and cream cheese to a microwave safe bowl
  2. Roughly mix then ingredients and then microwave for 30 seconds
  3. Stir ingredients together
  4. Add egg to mix
  5. Thoroughly mix ingredients

Rolling
  • Parchment Paper
  • Cooking Spray / Butter
  • Dough
  1. Grease two pieces of parchment paper using either cooking spray or butter
  2. Place dough between the two sheets
  3. Using a rolling pin, gently roll the dough into a circular shape < 1/4" thick
  4. Remove top parchment paper and poke flat dough several times with a fork

Baking Part I
  • Prepared Dough
  1. Place Dough in Oven
  2. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the dough turns a mottled golden brown
  3. Take out of oven

Toppings
  • Cooked Dough
  • Bacon and Onion mixture
  • 1 cup of Mozzarella Cheese
  1. Evenly spread bacon and onion mixture on the cooked dough
  2. Cover mixture in mozzarella cheese

Baking Part II
  • Incomplete pizza from previous step
  1. Return freshly topped pizza to oven
  2. Bake for another 8-10 minutes
  3. Remove from the oven when cheese on top begins to brown
  4. Let cool for several minutes before serving
